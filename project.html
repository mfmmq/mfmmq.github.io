Projects

I'm currently working on making a sleeptalking recorder in Python. This is to automatically record and save sleeptalking by connecting a raspberry pi to a high-quality microphone. The sleeptalking recorder Python backend is broken up into a poller, processor, and Flask api. The frontend is mainly JavaScript, HTML, and Bootstrap CSS.
The end goal is to get this continuously delivered to our Raspberry Pi by perhaps Docker image.

CFD-Assisted Study of Diesel Emission Generation
Engines and Combustion Group, University of Oxford, 2017-2018

While experimental results are often more indicative of engine characteristics and performance than numerical simulations, they are also far more costly to undertake and offer less of a view of internal workings. Through engine simulation, I analysed how different engine configurations affected performance and emission levels. This involed running test simulations, validating results with test engine data, and applying fundamental thermofluid theory to large datasets.

Ammonia Energy Storage Assessment
Department of Engineering, University of Oxford, 2016-2017

Designing an ammonia energy storage plant using industry-grade chemical engineering software and MATLAB and assessing its technical and economical feasibility.
